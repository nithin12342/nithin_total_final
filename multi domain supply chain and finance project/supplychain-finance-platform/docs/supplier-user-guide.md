# Supplier User Guide

## Overview

This guide provides instructions for suppliers using the Supply Chain Finance Platform. As a supplier, you can manage your products, process orders, track shipments, and access financial services through this platform.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Supplier Dashboard

1. Navigate to the supplier portal URL: `https://supplier.supplychain-finance.com`
2. Enter your supplier credentials
3. Complete multi-factor authentication if enabled
4. You will be redirected to the supplier dashboard

### Supplier Dashboard Overview

The supplier dashboard consists of several key sections:

1. **Overview** - Summary of your business metrics and recent activity
2. **Products** - Manage your product catalog
3. **Orders** - View and process customer orders
4. **Inventory** - Track and manage your inventory levels
5. **Shipments** - Track and manage outgoing shipments
6. **Invoices** - Create and manage invoices
7. **Analytics** - View business insights and forecasts
8. **Financing** - Access supply chain financing options

## Product Management

### Adding New Products

To add a new product to your catalog:

1. Click on the "Products" tab in the navigation menu
2. Click the "Add Product" button
3. Fill in the product details:
   - Product name
   - Description
   - SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)
   - Category
   - Unit price
   - Minimum order quantity
   - Lead time
4. Upload product images if available
5. Click "Save Product"

### Editing Product Information

To edit an existing product:

1. Navigate to the Products page
2. Find the product in the list
3. Click the "Edit" button next to the product
4. Make the necessary changes
5. Click "Save Changes"

### Removing Products

To remove a product from your catalog:

1. Navigate to the Products page
2. Find the product in the list
3. Click the "Delete" button next to the product
4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog box

Note: You can only delete products that are not currently part of any active orders.

## Order Management

### Viewing Orders

The Orders page displays all customer orders with their status:

- **Pending**: New orders awaiting confirmation
- **Confirmed**: Orders accepted and being processed
- **Shipped**: Orders that have been dispatched
- **Delivered**: Orders successfully delivered
- **Cancelled**: Orders that were cancelled

Each order shows:
- Order ID
- Customer information
- Order date
- Total amount
- Current status
- Expected delivery date

### Processing Orders

To process a new order:

1. Navigate to the Orders page
2. Find the order with "Pending" status
3. Click the "Process" button
4. Confirm product availability
5. Update the order status to "Confirmed"
6. Prepare the items for shipment

### Updating Order Status

To update an order's status:

1. Navigate to the Orders page
2. Find the relevant order
3. Click the "Update Status" button
4. Select the new status from the dropdown
5. Add any relevant notes
6. Click "Save Changes"

## Inventory Management

### Viewing Inventory

The Inventory page displays all your products with their current stock levels:

- Product name and SKU
- Current quantity in stock
- Reserved quantity (allocated to orders)
- Available quantity
- Last updated timestamp
- Reorder level (if set)

### Updating Inventory Levels

To update inventory levels:

1. Navigate to the Inventory page
2. Find the product you want to update
3. Click the "Edit" button next to the product
4. Enter the new quantity
5. Add a note explaining the change (optional)
6. Click "Save Changes"

### Setting Reorder Levels

To set automatic reorder alerts:

1. Navigate to the Inventory page
2. Find the product
3. Click the "Edit" button
4. Enter the reorder level quantity
5. Save the changes

When inventory falls below this level, you'll receive an alert.

### Inventory Analytics

The platform provides inventory analytics including:

- Stock turnover rates
- Seasonal demand patterns
- Low stock alerts
- Overstock warnings
- ABC analysis (categorization by value)

## Shipment Management

### Creating Shipments

To create a new shipment:

1. Navigate to the Shipments page
2. Click the "Create Shipment" button
3. Select the order(s) to include in the shipment
4. Enter shipping details:
   - Carrier information
   - Tracking number
   - Shipping address
   - Estimated delivery date
5. Add package details (weight, dimensions)
6. Click "Create Shipment"

### Tracking Shipments

To track an existing shipment:

1. Navigate to the Shipments page
2. Find the shipment in the list
3. Click on the shipment ID to view details
4. View the tracking history and current status

### Updating Shipment Status

To update a shipment's status:

1. Navigate to the Shipments page
2. Find the relevant shipment
3. Click the "Update Status" button
4. Select the new status:
   - In Transit
   - Out for Delivery
   - Delivered
   - Delayed
   - Exception
5. Add any relevant notes
6. Click "Save Changes"

## Invoice Management

### Creating Invoices

To create an invoice for an order:

1. Navigate to the Invoices page
2. Click the "Create Invoice" button
3. Select the order to invoice
4. Review the invoice details:
   - Line items
   - Quantities
   - Prices
   - Taxes
5. Enter payment terms
6. Click "Generate Invoice"

### Viewing Invoices

The Invoices page displays all your invoices with their status:

- **Draft**: Invoices being prepared
- **Pending**: Invoices sent to customers
- **Approved**: Invoices approved by customers
- **Financed**: Invoices funded through supply chain finance
- **Paid**: Invoices that have been paid
- **Overdue**: Invoices past their due date
- **Cancelled**: Invoices that were cancelled

### Invoice Financing

To request financing for an approved invoice:

1. Navigate to the Invoices page
2. Find the invoice with "Approved" status
3. Click the "Request Financing" button
4. Review financing terms:
   - Advance rate
   - Fees
   - Repayment terms
5. Accept the terms and submit the request

Once approved, funds will be deposited into your account within 24-48 hours.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

1. **Order Processing Delays**
   - Check inventory availability
   - Verify customer information
   - Contact customer service if needed

2. **Shipment Tracking Issues**
   - Verify tracking number accuracy
   - Contact carrier directly
   - Update tracking information in the system

3. **Invoice Disputes**
   - Review order details
   - Communicate with the customer
   - Provide supporting documentation

4. **Financing Request Rejections**
   - Review financing criteria
   - Update financial information
   - Contact financing partner for details
