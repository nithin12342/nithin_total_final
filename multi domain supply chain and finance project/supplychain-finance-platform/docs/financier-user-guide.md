# Financier User Guide

## Overview

This guide provides instructions for financiers using the Supply Chain Finance Platform. As a financier, you can evaluate financing requests, manage investments, monitor portfolio performance, and access advanced analytics to make informed decisions.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Financier Dashboard

1. Navigate to the financier portal URL: `https://financier.supplychain-finance.com`
2. Enter your financier credentials
3. Complete multi-factor authentication if enabled
4. You will be redirected to the financier dashboard

### Financier Dashboard Overview

The financier dashboard consists of several key sections:

1. **Portfolio Overview** - Summary of your investment portfolio and performance metrics
2. **Financing Requests** - Review and process new financing applications
3. **Active Investments** - Manage ongoing financing arrangements
4. **Risk Management** - Monitor and assess investment risks
5. **Analytics** - Access advanced analytics and market insights
6. **Reports** - Generate and view financial reports
7. **Compliance** - Manage regulatory and compliance requirements

## Portfolio Management

### Portfolio Overview

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your investment portfolio:

- Total portfolio value
- Current return on investment (ROI)
- Risk-weighted returns
- Diversification metrics
- Performance trends
- Cash flow projections

### Investment Categories

The platform supports various investment types:

1. **Invoice Financing**
   - Short-term financing for approved invoices
   - Advance rates typically 80-90%
   - Terms usually 30-90 days

2. **Inventory Financing**
   - Financing for inventory purchases
   - Advance rates typically 50-70%
   - Secured by inventory assets

3. **Purchase Order Financing**
   - Financing for large purchase orders
   - Advance rates typically 70-80%
   - Based on creditworthiness of buyers

4. **Export Financing**
   - Financing for international transactions
   - Specialized risk assessment
   - Currency hedging options

## Financing Request Management

### Viewing Financing Requests

The Financing Requests page displays all new applications:

- Request ID
- Supplier information
- Financing type
- Requested amount
- Proposed terms
- Risk score
- Application date
- Status

### Evaluating Requests

To evaluate a financing request:

1. Navigate to the Financing Requests page
2. Click on the request ID to view details
3. Review supplier profile and financials
4. Analyze risk assessment provided by the platform
5. Examine supporting documentation
6. Make a decision to approve, reject, or request additional information

### Risk Assessment Factors

The platform provides comprehensive risk analysis including:

- Supplier credit score
- Historical payment performance
- Financial health indicators
- Industry risk factors
- Geographic risk considerations
- Buyer creditworthiness (for invoice financing)
- Collateral valuation (for asset-based financing)

### Approval Workflow

To approve a financing request:

1. Review all documentation
2. Verify terms and conditions
3. Set final terms (interest rate, fees, repayment schedule)
4. Click "Approve" and confirm
5. The supplier will be notified of approval
6. Funds will be disbursed according to the agreed schedule

### Rejection Process

To reject a financing request:

1. Select valid reason for rejection from dropdown
2. Add detailed explanation (required)
3. Click "Reject" and confirm
4. The supplier will be notified with your feedback

## Active Investment Management

### Monitoring Investments

The Active Investments page displays all ongoing financing arrangements:

- Investment ID
- Supplier information
- Original amount
- Current balance
- Interest rate
- Repayment schedule
- Next payment date
- Status

### Investment Status Types

- **Active**: Currently accruing interest, payments current
- **Delinquent**: Missed payments, collection process initiated
- **Repaid**: Fully repaid, closed
- **Defaulted**: Unrecoverable, written off
- **Restructured**: Terms modified due to financial difficulty

### Payment Tracking

Monitor payment activity for each investment:

- Payment history
- Upcoming payments
- Missed payments
- Late fees assessed
- Collection activities

### Early Repayment

Handle early repayment requests:

1. Review request details
2. Calculate payoff amount (including any prepayment penalties)
3. Approve or reject the request
4. Process the payment
5. Close the investment if fully repaid

### Risk Mitigation Tools

Available risk management features:

1. **Diversification Alerts**

2. **Early Warning System**
   - Automated alerts for deteriorating credit conditions
   - Supplier performance degradation indicators

3. **Collateral Management**

   - Margin call functionality
   - Liquidation procedures

### Credit Limit Management

Set and manage credit limits for suppliers:

1. Navigate to the Credit Limits section
2. View current limits by supplier
3. Adjust limits based on performance and risk assessment
4. Set automatic review dates
5. Monitor limit utilization

## Best Practices

1. Diversify your portfolio across suppliers, industries, and geographies
2. Set appropriate credit limits based on risk assessment
3. Monitor investments actively and respond to early warning signals
4. Maintain adequate reserves for potential losses
5. Regularly review and adjust investment strategies
6. Stay informed about market trends and economic conditions

### Risk Management Best Practices

1. Implement robust due diligence processes
2. Use multiple risk assessment factors
3. Monitor concentration risks
4. Establish clear approval workflows
5. Maintain comprehensive documentation
6. Regularly stress-test your portfolio

### Compliance Best Practices

1. Stay current with regulatory requirements
2. Maintain detailed audit trails
3. Conduct regular compliance reviews
4. Provide ongoing staff training

### Operational Best Practices
