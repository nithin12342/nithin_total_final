# Buyer User Guide

## Overview

This guide provides instructions for buyers using the Supply Chain Finance Platform. As a buyer, you can discover suppliers, place orders, track shipments, manage invoices, and optimize your procurement processes through this platform.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Buyer Dashboard

1. Navigate to the buyer portal URL: `https://buyer.supplychain-finance.com`
2. Enter your buyer credentials
3. Complete multi-factor authentication if enabled
4. You will be redirected to the buyer dashboard

### Buyer Dashboard Overview

The buyer dashboard consists of several key sections:

1. **Overview** - Summary of your purchasing activity and key metrics
2. **Suppliers** - Discover and manage relationships with suppliers
3. **Orders** - Create, track, and manage purchase orders
4. **Shipments** - Track incoming shipments
5. **Invoices** - Review and approve invoices
6. **Analytics** - Access purchasing insights and forecasts
7. **Financing** - Manage payment terms and financing options

## Supplier Management

### Discovering Suppliers

To discover new suppliers:

1. Click on the "Suppliers" tab in the navigation menu
2. Use the search and filter options to find suppliers by:
   - Product category
   - Geographic location
   - Certification status
   - Performance ratings
   - Pricing competitiveness
3. Review supplier profiles and product catalogs
4. Compare suppliers based on your criteria

### Supplier Profiles

Each supplier profile includes:

- Company information and contact details
- Product catalog with pricing
- Performance metrics (on-time delivery, quality ratings)
- Financial health indicators
- Certifications and compliance status
- Customer reviews and ratings

### Managing Supplier Relationships

To manage existing supplier relationships:

1. Navigate to the Suppliers page
2. View your approved suppliers list
3. Review performance metrics regularly
4. Communicate through the platform's messaging system
5. Provide feedback on supplier performance
6. Renew or terminate supplier agreements as needed

## Order Management

### Creating Purchase Orders

To create a new purchase order:

1. Click on the "Orders" tab
2. Click the "Create Order" button
3. Select supplier from your approved list
4. Add products to the order:
   - Search by product name or SKU
   - Specify quantities
   - Review unit prices
   - Add special instructions if needed
5. Set delivery requirements:
   - Delivery date
   - Shipping address
   - Special handling instructions
6. Review order summary and total amount
7. Submit the order

### Order Status Tracking

Order statuses include:

- **Draft**: Order being prepared
- **Submitted**: Order sent to supplier
- **Confirmed**: Supplier has accepted the order
- **Processing**: Supplier is preparing the order
- **Shipped**: Order has been dispatched
- **Delivered**: Order has been received
- **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled
- **On Hold**: Order is pending resolution of an issue

### Order Modifications

To modify an existing order:

1. Navigate to the Orders page
2. Find the order (must be in Draft or Submitted status)
3. Click the "Edit" button
4. Make necessary changes
5. Save the updated order

Note: Once an order is confirmed by the supplier, modifications may require supplier approval.

### Order History

The order history provides:

- Complete record of all past orders
- Search and filter capabilities
- Export options for procurement reporting
- Spend analysis by supplier, category, and time period

## Shipment Tracking

### Monitoring Incoming Shipments

To track your shipments:

1. Navigate to the "Shipments" tab
2. View all incoming shipments with their status
3. Click on a shipment ID for detailed tracking information
4. Receive notifications about shipment status changes

### Shipment Status Types

- **Pending**: Shipment scheduled but not yet dispatched
- **In Transit**: Shipment is moving toward destination
- **Out for Delivery**: Shipment is with local delivery service
- **Delivered**: Shipment has been received
- **Delayed**: Shipment is behind schedule
- **Exception**: Shipment has encountered an issue

### Receiving Shipments

To record receipt of shipments:

1. When shipment arrives, navigate to the Shipments page
2. Find the shipment in the list
3. Click "Confirm Receipt"
4. Verify quantities and condition of goods
5. Report any discrepancies or damages
6. Complete the receiving process

## Invoice Management

### Reviewing Invoices

To review incoming invoices:

1. Navigate to the "Invoices" tab
2. View all invoices with their status
3. Click on an invoice ID to review details:
   - Line items and quantities
   - Unit prices and totals
   - Tax calculations
   - Payment terms
4. Verify invoice accuracy against purchase orders and receipts

### Invoice Status Types

- **Received**: New invoice awaiting review
- **Under Review**: Invoice being verified
- **Approved**: Invoice approved for payment
- **Rejected**: Invoice disputed or incorrect
- **Paid**: Invoice has been paid
- **Overdue**: Payment past due date
- **Cancelled**: Invoice was cancelled

### Approving Invoices

To approve an invoice:

1. Review invoice details carefully
2. Verify against purchase order and receipt
3. Click "Approve" if everything is correct
4. The invoice will be queued for payment processing

### Rejecting Invoices

To reject an invoice:

1. Identify the issue with the invoice
2. Click "Reject"
3. Provide detailed reason for rejection
4. The supplier will be notified to resolve the issue

### Early Payment Options

Take advantage of early payment discounts:

1. Review payment terms on approved invoices
2. Identify invoices with early payment discounts
3. Opt for early payment through the platform
4. Receive automatic discount processing

### Payment Terms Management

3. Receive alerts when approaching budget limits

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

1. **Order Discrepancies**
   - Verify order details with supplier
   - Check for pricing or specification changes
   - Communicate through the platform messaging system

2. **Shipment Delays**
   - Track shipment status through carrier systems
   - Contact supplier for updates
   - Consider alternative suppliers for urgent needs

3. **Invoice Disputes**
   - Review purchase order and receipt documentation
   - Communicate discrepancy to supplier
   - Use platform dispute resolution tools

4. **Supplier Performance Issues**
   - Document performance problems
   - Communicate concerns to supplier
   - Consider supplier replacement if issues persist
